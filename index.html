As of February 2023, Dhruv Rathee began a shorts channel to share 30-seconds fact videos. This channel offers a quick and accessible way for viewers to engage with the information Rathee presents.[10] In September 2022, he faced controversy when a video he posted about the political crisis in Pakistan was blocked by India's Ministry of Information and Broadcasting. The video contained a distorted map of India, in which parts of Kashmir were depicted as part of Pakistan or "disputed" and in March 2023, he also faced copyright charges from Dabur on his video titled "The dark-side of cold drinks". Calcutta high court ordered to remove the video.
